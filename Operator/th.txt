There are three operator:
1. unary operator
    - it will required one operand

2. binary operator
    - it will required two operands

3. ternary operator
    - it will required three operands

binary : 
1. Airthmatic operator : + , - , * , / , % , ** 
2. Assignment operator : = , += , -= , *= , %= , /= 
3. logical operator : && , || , ! 
4. bitwise operator : & , | , ^ 
5. relational operator : == , <= , >= , === , != , !== 
6. shift operator : << , >>
